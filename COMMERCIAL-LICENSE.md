## Overview

Zenith Grid is released under the GNU Affero General Public License v3 (AGPL v3). However, we offer commercial licensing for organizations that wish to:

- Use Zenith Grid in proprietary/closed-source products
- Offer Zenith Grid as a managed service without open-sourcing modifications

## What the AGPL v3 Requires

Under AGPL v3, if you modify Zenith Grid and offer it as a service (even internally), you must:

1. Open source all your modifications
2. Provide the complete source code to all users
3. License your modifications under AGPL v3
